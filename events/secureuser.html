Course Description
About Penetration Testing

The importance of Cybersecurity in this modern era made Eyesec introduce a junior penetration tester course for those who want to dive deep into the world of Ethical Hacking. This course revolves around the basics of penetration testing and helps build a hacker mindset which is key to being a better pentester.


Penetration tester/ Red teamer plays an important role in an organization where his role is to find critical vulnerabilities and safeguard the organization’s infrastructure from malicious actors. Junior Penetration tester course helps you understand the core fundamentals of penetration testing.

This course is for anyone who is interested in becoming a Penetration Tester no matter your current skill set. The curriculum is designed for absolute beginners interested in a career as a security professional. This course is highly practical but it won't neglect the theory.

To develop the mindset of a Black Hat Hacker and evolve as a Penetration Tester, you need to get good at the things listed below:

  • Should think out of the box.
  • Should be open to learn new topics regularly.
  • Master researching at your end for all the covered topics(This is IMP!).

FAQs
Do i need to know something before joining the course?

No, The Junior Penetration Testing course covers all the topics required to be a red teamer

Can I join the training and join later as an intern??

Yes, you can convert the training to an internship as long as you complete the practical labs and submit the report as an internship project report.

Do I need coding experience?

Coding is not required for this course, you might some coding to develop exploits but it’s completely different from conventional coding.

I already had a pen testing experience. Do I still need to attend the training to get the certification?

No, if you have pen testing experience you can attempt the exam without training.

What tools are needed to attend training?

All the tools and labs required for the course will be provided at the beginning of the course

Is there any refund policy?

As of now, we don’t offer a refund.

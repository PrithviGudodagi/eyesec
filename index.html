About us

We are a team of security-focused, passionate professionals. Our team comprises IT professionals, academicians, researchers, and students who are committed to contributing towards a cyber-secure society.
Our team acts as a shield between cybercriminals and organizations and people We believe that prevention is better than cure philosophy and we work towards preventing cybercrime and mitigating the associated risks by providing cyber security services such as:

Vulnerability Analysis and Penetration Testing of Applications

Installing SSL Certificates

Data backup and Recovery,

Secure coding in Software Development.

Sometimes we cannot prevent cyber crimes, but we certainly provide assistance to the people and organizations as first responders.
